# How To Contribute?

To contribute, you must have the following... 

1. A working Godot engine (wether Godot, Godot Mobile, Xogot (Recommend paying one time)) it all has to be connected.
2. Git, to push to github.

When updating the code. You must always describe your changes in the files, thats a requirement (unless an eligible contributor)

Super simple. Quick and easy.
